Welcome to the first edition of the High AI-Qatalyst series. We aren't just adding AI to our workflow; we are re-architecting how we build. This fortnight, we move beyond "chatting with AI" to Spec-Driven Development (SDD).
 
The Core Concepts
  • Spec-Driven Development (SDD): Stop jumping straight to code. In an AI-first world, the Specification is the source of truth. If the spec is rigorous, the AI implements it perfectly.
  • BMAD (Breakthrough Method for Agile AI-Driven Development): A framework that uses specialized AI personas (Analyst, Architect, Coder) to orchestrate the entire lifecycle.
